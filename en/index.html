HL - Interpretation

My name is Hanna Lehrmann and I have developed the concept HL-Interpretation. I guarantee interpreting and teaching at a high professional level, based on more than 30 years of experience as a consecutive and simultaneous interpreter.

  • Interpreting for prominent Danish and Polish politicians as well as for private companies, trade unions and public institutions in Denmark
  • Teaching consecutive and simultaneous interpreting at Copenhagen Business College
  • Examining candidates for sworn translators between Danish and Polish at Copenhagen Business College
